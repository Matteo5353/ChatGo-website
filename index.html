What Is ChatGo

A solution for when you don't know what to do in a city.

ChatGo is for friends, travellers, locals and families discovering cities. Route planning, saved places, and recommendations stay in one flow.

Start a tour from a selected point, keep notes on map pins, and share a group link to collaborate quickly.

Why teams use it

  • Pick a city and begin a route in under a minute
  • Add favorite places as public or private entries
  • Keep one source of truth for plans and updates
  • Invite others with a group link and explore together

Live Map Interactions

Tap on the map to pick starting points, add custom stops, and shape routes in seconds.

Smart City Search

Jump to any city quickly and explore nearby areas without reworking your full plan.

Budget-Friendly Filters

Set a maximum price so recommendations stay aligned with your group's budget.

Planning Workspace

Keep your planning context in one place while comparing options and adjusting your route.

Public and Private Places

Decide if a place should be visible to everyone or kept private to your group of friends.

Group Link Collaboration

Share a group link to share the places you add or join one to see places others have added.

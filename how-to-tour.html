Tutorial 1

How to follow a tour

Back To Tutorials Watch 60s Tutorial
01

Ask tour

Ask for a tour

Set duration, choose the city, and select interests so recommendations match your intent.

Clear inputs at this stage improve suggestion quality and reduce manual edits later.

02

Start point

Select start point

Choose where the tour begins, either manually or from your current position.

Pay attention: if the selected location is far from where the tour is being generated, it may fail to create a tour.

03

Tour ready

Review generated tour

The service returns a route you can follow immediately as your base plan.

Check stop order and timing before sharing it with your group.

If places do not appear immediately, wait a few seconds while content finishes loading.

04

Edit route

Edit route

Add new places or remove stops to better fit your available time and goals.

05

Details

Check place details

Open detailed place info and leave a kudos when you like a recommendation.

This helps surface useful places and improves future recommendation signals.

06

XP

Track your XP

Gain XP while exploring tours and interacting with places inside the app.

XP offers quick progress feedback and keeps the experience engaging over time.

07

Feedback

Leave feedback

Your feedback is valuable and helps improve the service over time.
